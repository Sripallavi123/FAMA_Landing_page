Our Story

5+ Years of Excellence in Grooming

Established in 2018, Fama Barber Shop & Beauty Salon has been providing premium grooming and beauty services to the community of Denton, TX. Our mission is to deliver exceptional haircuts, grooming, and beauty treatments in a welcoming, classic environment that caters to both men and women. We take pride in our attention to detail and personalized service, ensuring each client leaves looking and feeling their best. Our skilled barbers and beauty specialists combine traditional techniques with modern trends to create custom styles that suit each individual’s personality and lifestyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

We accept both appointments and walk-ins! However, we recommend booking in advance to guarantee your preferred time slot with our master barbers.

How long does a typical haircut take?

A standard haircut takes about 30-45 minutes. More intricate styles or full grooming packages may take up to 60 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, AMEX), and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay).

Do you offer loyalty programs or discounts?

Yes! Our loyalty program gives you 1 free haircut after every 10 visits. We also offer 15% student discounts with valid ID.

What products do you use/sell?

We use premium brands like American Crew, Suavecito, and Reuzel. All products are available for purchase at our shop.
